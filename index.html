Liam Caulfield

Passionate about data and the great outdoors, I am an experienced data professional with expertise in Earth Sciences, Political Science, and Data Science. Combining my love for nature with my skills in data management and analysis, I create impact in environmental conservation and foster access to green spaces for all.

As a Wilderness and Interpretive Park Ranger, I utilized geospatial analysis to protect national parks and inspire environmental stewardship. In my role as Wilderness Programs Manager at NatureBridge, I expanded access to environmental education programs for historically marginalized communities.

Currently pursuing an M.S. in Information Management with a specialization in Data Science, I developed visualization and reporting infrastructure using Power BI as a Database Associate. I facilitated the transition to Engaging Networks CRM, ensuring a smooth migration process.

I specialize in machine learning applications for econometrics, aiming to develop robust models for forecasting, policy analysis, and sustainable economic practices. By incorporating environmental factors into decision-making, I strive to create a future where sustainability and equity are paramount.

With a diverse background and multidisciplinary approach, I leverage data-driven solutions to protect our natural world and promote equitable access to green spaces for all.
