Renewable Energy/ML Projects

Wind Power Prediction with Keras

In this portfolio project, I will use Keras, a powerful deep learning library, to build a wind power prediction model. By utilizing historical wind turbine data, including wind speed, wind direction, and previous power outputs, the model will forecast future energy generation, aiding in efficient energy resource planning and management. Through this project, I aim to demonstrate my proficiency in deep learning and its application in renewable energy forecasting.

Energy Consumption Forecasting with XGBoost

Development of a predictive model for hourly energy consumption using the PMJE hourly dataset. By employing the powerful XGBoost algorithm, we will build a robust model capable of capturing intricate patterns in energy consumption, which will aid in optimizing energy distribution and supporting effective decision-making in the energy sector. Ultimately, this project contributes to sustainable energy practices and more efficient resource utilization.

Wind Power Curve Modeling with Multiple Regression Techniques

In this portfolio project, we aim to model the wind power curve using five different regression techniques: KNeighbors, DecisionTree, ExtraTrees, RandomForest, and GradientBoosting. The dataset, derived from a wind turbine's SCADA system in Turkey, captures variables such as wind speed, direction, and generated power at 10-minute intervals. By comparing the performance of these regression techniques, we will identify the most effective model for predicting wind turbine performance, enabling us to optimize energy generation and gain valuable insights into wind power utilization in Turkey.

Machine Learning Project Portfolio

Improve steel plate quality assurance through the power of Neural Networks. Discover how machine learning can accurately predict various fault types in steel plates based on essential numerical features, enhancing safety and efficiency in manufacturing and construction industries.

Support Vector Machine - Portuguese Sea Battles

Delve into the historical naval clashes between Portuguese and Dutch/British ships from 1583 to 1663 through Support Vector Machine (SVM) analysis. Explore the intricacies of machine learning to gain valuable insights into battle outcomes, strategies, and historical significance, shedding light on the maritime history of the era.

Twitter Fake News Classifier - Naive Bayes & Neural Network

Embark on a fascinating journey of detecting fake news on Twitter as we compare the efficacy of Naive Bayes and Neural Network models. Explore the intricate world of natural language processing and machine learning to develop a powerful classifier, aiding in the fight against misinformation and enhancing the credibility of social media content.

Naive Bayes - Youtube Spam Classification

Tackle the ever-growing issue of spam in YouTube comments by developing a powerful Naive Bayes classifier. Dive into the realm of natural language processing and machine learning to distinguish between genuine comments and spam, enhancing the overall user experience and content quality on the platform.Five datasets were used, 4 to train the data and 1 to test - leading to a 80% training set and a 20% testing set.

Expectation - Maximization Clustering - Country Life Cycle Savings Analysis

Discover the world's economic diversity and savings behavior as we apply Expectation-Maximization (EM) Clustering to analyze country life cycle savings data.

Agglomerative and Divisive Clustering - Airline Safety Records

Delve into the realm of airline safety records using Agglomerative and Divisive Clustering techniques to uncover hidden patterns and trends. Analyze safety data from airlines worldwide, revealing valuable insights that can shape the future of aviation safety practices.

Decision Trees and Random Forest - “Blues Guitarists Hand Posture and Thumbing Style

Decision tree and random forest algorithms to analyze the "Blues Guitarists Hand Posture and Thumbing Style by Region and Birth Period" dataset. By using hand postures and thumb styles as features, I accurately predicted the birth year of blues guitarists. Additionally, I explored the impact of including the region as an additional feature in the model evaluation.

Logistic Regression and kNN - Post Quality

Classifying post quality as either good or bad using two popular machine learning algorithms: Logistic Regression and k-Nearest Neighbors (kNN). By analyzing various features of the posts, the models aim to determine the likelihood of a post being of high or low quality, providing valuable insights for content moderation and enhancing user experience on the platform.
